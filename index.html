The Science

What I found when I started researching.

When I started digging into the research, what struck me first was a Harvard study by neuroscientist Sara Lazar. She found structural brain changes in subjects who meditated 27 minutes a day for 8 weeks. Grey matter increased in areas linked to learning and memory. It decreased in areas tied to stress and anxiety. The brain physically changed. In eight weeks.

Then I learned about binaural beat technology. It delivers specific frequencies through headphones. Each ear receives a slightly different tone, and your brain synchronizes to the target frequency. This is how you can access deep meditation states without years of practice. For someone like me, who struggled to quiet my mind, this was a turning point.
