The Science

Your brain operates on frequencies. Here's why that matters.

Harvard neuroscientist Sara Lazar found structural brain changes in subjects who meditated 27 minutes a day for 8 weeks. Grey matter increased in areas linked to learning and memory. It decreased in areas tied to stress and anxiety. The brain physically changed.

Binaural beat technology delivers specific frequencies through headphones. Each ear receives a slightly different tone. Your brain synchronizes to the target frequency. This is how you access deep meditation states without years of practice.

Beta

13 - 30 Hz

Waking state. Active thinking and concentration.

Alpha

8 - 13 Hz

Calm focus. Relaxed awareness and creativity.

Theta

4 - 8 Hz

Deep meditation. Subconscious access and reprogramming.

Delta

0.5 - 4 Hz

Deep sleep. Healing, regeneration, and restoration.
